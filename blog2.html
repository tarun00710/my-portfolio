Motivation is over-rated

You may lose motivation but remember you need to be deciplined.By being deciplined you will have a sense of accompanishment every down and you wont be further feeling down and giving up.Things can get tough ,motivation can fade ,It always does but if you keep doing your work and be honest ,the motivation and the positive energy will rise because you never gaveup and you are probably better than yesterday.

So,do you really need to be always motivated inorder to do something?

No, Your just need to continue if you know you can achieve your goal and thats what really you wanted to do. Remind yourself...how far you have came and how were you before you started and at beginning.

Drive your head back and think how far and better you have come to be.
